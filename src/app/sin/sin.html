What is a SIN?

A Social Insurance Number (SIN) is a 9-digit number you may need to work in Canada, pay taxes, or access government programs.

When do you need it?

  • Starting a job
  • Filing taxes
  • Accessing eligible government benefits

Important

  • Your SIN is free
  • Only share it when legally required
  • This guide helps you prepare — it is not an official government service
